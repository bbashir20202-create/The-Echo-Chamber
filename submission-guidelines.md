# How to Submit an Opinion

### Rules (Read Carefully)

1. You must submit an opinion you **actually believe**.
2. The opinion should be controversial, ridiculous, or emotionally charged.
3. No hate speech, no racism, no targeted attacks on real individuals.
4. Be creative. The stupider the opinion, the better the challenge.

### How to Submit:

1. Click on the `opinions/` folder
2. Click "Add file" → "Create new file"
3. Name the file in this format:
   `your-opinion-in-kebab-case.md`
   (Example: `pineapple-on-pizza-is-disgusting.md`)

4. Copy the format from [`submission-template.md`](./submission-template.md)
5. Fill in your opinion
6. Open a Pull Request with the title: `[NEW OPINION] Your Opinion Here`

---

### What Makes a Good Submission?

- Strong emotional charge
- Widely debated topic
- Slight absurdity (the best ones make people say “Wait… that’s actually insane”)

**Current Champion Opinion:** “Pineapple belongs on pizza” — still causing existential crises since May 2026.
