# The Echo Chamber Manifesto

We live in the most opinionated time in human history.

Everyone is screaming.  
No one is listening.

So we built this place.

**The Echo Chamber** is a sacred temple of intellectual masochism.

The rules are simple and brutal:

- You must defend the opposite of what you believe.
- You are not allowed to be sarcastic.
- You must argue with sincerity, creativity, and effort.
- The more you hate the opinion, the harder you must try to defend it.

This is not a debate club.

This is **exposure therapy** with extra steps.

We are here to train the muscle most people refuse to use:  
**Understanding the other side without secretly wanting to punch them.**

Every time you write a passionate defense of something you despise, a small part of your brain dies and is reborn slightly less stupid.

Welcome to The Echo Chamber.

Please leave your ego at the door.  
It will not survive the experience.

— The Management
